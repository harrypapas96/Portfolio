Disability Recreation & Sports SA

I was asked by the local South Australian charity Disability Recreation & Sports SA (formally known as Wheelchair Sports) to assist them with freelance design work.

The tasks they requested consisted of:
• Creating an email information letter with a donation pledge form for their current campaigns (coded using HTML)
• Creating an email donation receipt (coded using HTML)
• Making edits to their existing website
• Filming and editing a promotional video for their ‘Wheelchair Footy’ campaign
